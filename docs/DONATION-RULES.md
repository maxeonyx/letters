# NZ Electoral Donation Rules

Research needed on how donations to MPs work in New Zealand.

## Key Questions

1. Can you donate directly to individual MPs (not parties)?
2. What are the disclosure thresholds?
3. Are there limits on donation amounts?
4. What are the rules for anonymous donations?
5. Does donating via cash-in-envelope have different rules?

## Current Understanding

> "It's inconsistently possible - most MPs *don't* have public information on this, but some electorate MPs do."

This means:
- Some electorate MPs have donation mechanisms on their websites
- Many do not
- Party donations are more standardized
- Cash in physical letter is operationally simple but may have reporting implications
