Why I'm Writing to Every MP in Parliament

Max Clarke — February 2026

The Letter

I sent a letter and $50 to every Member of Parliament asking them to support international efforts to ensure AI benefits everyone.

The Core Concern

AI is advancing rapidly. Without global coordination, a few companies may benefit while many people lose the ability to provide value and earn income. New Zealand has a voice in international forums, and I believe we should use it.

About Me

I have a degree in Computer Science and a Master's in Deep Learning. I work as a software developer at the NZX. I run AI Safety Australia New Zealand meetups in Wellington. I am not affiliated with any political party.
